I believe we're shaped by the people, places, and moments we encounter along the way. This portfolio is an archive of those dots: past experiences, lessons, and constraints, connected across time to form who I am today. Btw, this is Rae Fan. I embrace ambiguity with grit, balance creativity with real-world e-commerce constraints, and hold the big picture while staying grounded in the details. Right now, my focus is on crafting digital experiences at the intersection of brand, systems, and commercial reality.
